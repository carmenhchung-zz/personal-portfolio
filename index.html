ABOUT CARMEN

(from a totally non-biased, third party perspective.)


Aussie born Carmen Chung graduated with a Bachelor of Arts/Bachelor of Laws (with Honors) from the University of New South Wales. She was a corporate lawyer working internationally for seven years before she had a late 20s identity crisis discovered her passion for coding.


She was awarded the Women in Tech Scholarship to study a Diploma of Information Technology at Coder Academy in Sydney (Australia), where she learnt HTML, CSS, Javascript, Ruby on Rails, React and Node.js.


Carmen is passionate about details and documentation, learning new things, trying to make the world a better place, and ice-cream. She currently works as a full stack software engineer at Valiant Finance.


Finally, don't challenge her to drinking games. Seriously. Don't.
