LIFESTYLE LOOKBOOK

We were assigned the task of re-designing a website of our choice.

As part of the project, we were asked to sketch our design on paper, create a Pinterest moodboard, select appropriate fonts and colours, and create a basic wireframe of the new design.

THE WEBSITE

The website I chose was a blog, Lifestyle Lookbook, which I co-run with my best friend.

We last revised our blog around one year ago - but it's in need of a fresh, uncluttered design that will encourage readers to continue browsing our blog.

THE DESIGN PROCESS

I researched other blogs that I liked and brainstormed ideas for the overall design.

My preference was for a minimalist design focused on images, with a bronze/gold, grey and white colour palette.

I then made a rough draft sketch on paper and created a Pinterest moodboard for font, colour and layout inspiration.


THE WIREFRAME

I designed a wireframe in Justinmind Prototyper.

The design was simple, reduced the menu navigation options, and focused more heavily on images rather than text. I also removed the right hand column (including the social media account previews).
