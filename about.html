ABOUT CARMEN

(from a totally non-biased, third party perspective.)

Aussie born Carmen Chung graduated from a Bachelor of Arts/Bachelor of Laws (with Honors) from the University of New South Wales. She was a corporate lawyer for seven years before she had a late 20s identity crisis discovered her passion for coding.

She was awarded the Women in Tech Scholarship to study a Diploma of Information Technology at the Coder Academy in Sydney (Australia), where she is currently learning HTML, CSS, and Ruby.

Carmen is passionate about details, learning new things, trying to make the world a better place, and ice-cream.
